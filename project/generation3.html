Generation 3

The third generation began in 1979 and ended in 1993. It introduced the Fox Body Mustang which was a redesign of the previous generation. The Fox Body Mustang kept the traditional long hood and shor rear deck but added European-style design. This included rectangular headlights and more cargo space.

This generation included a coupe, convertible, and hatchback. The trim levels included an unamed base model, Ghia, Cobra, L, Gl, GLX, GT, Turbo GT, LX, GT-250, SVO, and Cobra R. The engines carried over from the second generation, however it was troublesome and underwent improvements. In 1982, the Mustang GT returned and effectively replaced the Cobra while using a high-output 4.9-liter engine.
