Project Requirements and Design

Overview

The goal of the project is to create an interactive and informational site about Ford Mustangs. The target audiance is Ford Mustang enthusiasts who want to get all of their Ford Mustang facts in one place.

The content of the webpage will vary from page to page but most of the site will contain images, descriptions, and interactive elements to reveal fun Mustang facts! The goal with the content is to make it interactive, informative, and yet still fun to go through.

Wireframe and Site Map

Page Design

The following information will go into brief detail into which each page will contain

Home Page

This serves as the landing page for the website. This page should be intriguing to the user to incentivize them to stay for a while. It also gives the user a basic understanding of what the site will provide.

The content contained on this page will consist of a brief overview paragraph at the top of the page followed by a timeline that works from top to bottom displaying text and a picture to coincide with it. The images of the timeline will be sorted on different sides of the page (left to right) in order to make the overall design more appealing.

  • What actions will happen on the page?

    • Animation of images moving with the scroll feature

Generation Content Pages

There is six generations of Ford Mustangs that will be disscussed throughout the site and each of these pages contain similar layout. Namely, the content is what will be changing throughout each page. Therefore, the design of these six pages will follow the following design criteria.

The content on these pages will contain multiple images displayed in a slide show. There will be history facts sorted throughout the page and basic information on current selling price for a specific model. There will also be ways to learn more about the vehicles by outsourcing links to other sites with seller/buyer information.

  • What actions will happen on the page?
    • The navbar is included on all these pages but they maintain static

Dynamic Functionality

Due to the nature of the page, there is not much dynamic functionality to the page aside from a subscribe form and animation of images. The subscription feature will be a simple form seen on many sites, especially news sites, to have frequent users get daily updates and content from the site. The animation of images will dynamically change the page but the coding for it will be executed in CSS.
